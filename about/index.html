Hello
I'm Zach

Thank you for taking the time to check out my website! This is a place where I try to put all (or at least some) of my completed projects so that other people can see them. I also try to write a little bit on the blog here, though I can’t make any promises about the consistency of quality from post to post. What I’m interested in writing about varies wildly from month to month, so there is very little cohesion in theme.

As for myself, I a recent graduate of Swarthmore College, with degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science. I was awarded high honors through Swarthmore’s unique honors program, which was one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences of my life. This involved a series of seminars on graduate level math topics teaching both the academic maturity to self study and communication skills to participate in a highly collaborative setting. In my post college time I’m focusing mostly on finding work, but I continue to self study mathematics and work on personal programming projects. I intend to return to graduate school some time in the next couple of years to pursue a PhD in either mathematics or deep learning. For more information here is my resume.
